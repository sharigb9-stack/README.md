Instructions for running the project.

1. Open the application and go to the Login screen.
2. Enter you username and password and press Enter.
3. On the Ticket Sales screen, select the movie, showtime, and number of tickets. Press Next.
4. In the Concessions section, choose the desired products to accompany the movie.
   Press Next to continue.
5. Review all the information on the Purchase Checkout screen, making sure the details are correct.
6. Press confirm to finalize the purchase or back to correct any information.
7. Upon confirmation, the system completes the transaction and displays a thank you message.
